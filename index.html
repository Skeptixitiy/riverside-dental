Meet Your Cambridge Dentists

Dr. Carolyn Blair and Dr. Brian Stewart have been providing exceptional dental care in Cambridge since 1992. As a husband-and-wife team, they combine professional expertise with genuine care for their patients.

Both graduates of the University of Toronto Faculty of Dentistry, they opened Riverside Dental in downtown Cambridge over 30 years ago. Their philosophy centers on creating a welcoming, family-oriented practice where patients feel comfortable and informed.

Dr. Carolyn Blair & Dr. Brian Stewart

Education: University of Toronto Faculty of Dentistry, Class of 1992

Experience: Over 30 years serving the Cambridge community

Personal: Proud parents of three boys and devoted pet owners to their Yorkshire Terrier, Minnie

Philosophy: "We love treating families in the Cambridge area and helping them with all of their dental needs."

Our Dental Services

Comprehensive care for your entire family's dental needs

Preventive Care

Regular exams, cleanings, and digital X-rays to maintain optimal oral health and catch issues early.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers to enhance your natural beauty and confidence.

Restorative Dentistry

Fillings, crowns (including CEREC® same-day crowns), bridges, and dentures to restore function and appearance.

Emergency Care

Immediate attention for toothaches, broken teeth, and other dental emergencies with same-day appointments when possible.

General Dentistry

Comprehensive care including extractions, root canals, and periodontal treatment for complete oral health.

CEREC® Same-Day Crowns

Advanced technology allows us to create and place beautiful, durable crowns in just one visit—no temporary crowns or multiple appointments needed.
